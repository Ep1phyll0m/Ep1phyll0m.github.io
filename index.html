Artist Statement

Hello, welcome to my web portfolio! I'm Quynh Luong, and I'm curently a sophomore at SJSU looking to pursue a degree in Digital Media Art. As the major itself is rather expansive, I'd personally like to delve into more of the 3D modeling side and concept art development in general. I hail from Stockton, California located in the San Joaquin Delta Valley, and I've lived there for pretty much my whole life until SJSU. A lot of my work is based on my upbringing as a 2nd generation Vietnamese-American immigrant, whether that be directly inspired by Vietnamese aesthetics or the culture in general in regards to my observations on religion and tradition in general.
