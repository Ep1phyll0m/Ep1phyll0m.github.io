Artist Statement

Quynh Luong is curently a senior at SJSU looking to pursue a degree in Digital Media Art and a minor in Political Science. He is interested in telling stories through any medium, whether that be through 3D modeling or illustration. Hailing from Stockton, California, located in the San Joaquin Delta Valley, a lot of his work has been based on his upbringing as a 2nd generation Vietnamese-American immigrant, whether that be directly inspired by Vietnamese aesthetics or the culture in general in regards to his personal observations on religion and tradition in general.
